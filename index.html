About me

“Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler.”

My name is Márton Kocsis and I am a digital product designer working in Amsterdam, North Holland. I use my creativity through the problem-solving process of developing purposeful and innovative solutions that embody functional and aesthetic demands based on the needs of the intended user.

I'm a friendly, honest and energetic person. I believe that being organized, precise and determined are the keys to reaching all of my professional and personal goals.

In 1994, I met my first graphical user interface. 10 years later, I made my very first image in Photoshop at age 13. From that moment, it became a hobby.

I studied information technology then eventually I graduated in marketing in 2011. In the next couple of years, along with my curiosity, I discovered and tried many creative fields when I was writing my diploma work in multimedia and branding. Besides my studies, I was also selling my creativity as a hobbyist.

In 2014, I moved to the Netherlands. I got the great opportunity to study more about the creative industry. This period of my career has completely changed my perception about design and aided me to understand better how people perceive the world.

I am constantly looking for new challenges with the same enthusiasm when I was young. I am mainly focused on creating beautiful user interface elements and taking care of user experience problems. I have recently started to study 3D and I am looking forward to seeing what the future holds.

I like to spend my free time with my family and my hobbies.

When it comes to working, I always aim to be precise and accurate. I consider myself focused, hard-working and passionate about design. I like to think outside the box and whenever I can, I take the opportunity to learn something new.

My career goal is to become an acknowledged designer, a member of a team where I can explore more of my creativity and accomplish success with my input.

Product Design / UI / UX

Gymtionary Workout App

The Gymtionary workout application is one of my pet projects. It has been created only for my entertainment and to stay sane during the Covid-19 lockdown. I'm a big fan of bodyweight workouts, so the simple idea of having versatile workout routines gave me the idea of combining the dictionary with the sport.

The concept of the application is simple. Each letter of the alphabet is an exercise, and each word in the dictionary is a workout session.

I chose minimalism and bicolour design. Building a page for statistics, performance history and other popular workout features are among my future plans.

Package / Graphic Design

Atlas Salt

Atlas Salt project is also one of my exam projects when I was studying design. The reason to keep it in highlight, because since then I haven't had the chance to work on a project like this on my own from the really beginning to the very end yet. It taught me a lot about working with different materials and folding techniques.

I have spent most of my time researching, sketching, prototyping, and reading about package design and the topic. In the rest, I've designed the brand meanwhile I had been failing miserably during the building process. The glue flaps were too weak and the substrate was too thin to hold the weight of the salt. Correcting what I had to, I've assembled a working version and shot some photos. I don't even need to say, there's still a ton to learn.
